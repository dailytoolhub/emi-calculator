How EMI is Calculated (2026 Guide)

An Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is the fixed monthly payment you make to a bank or NBFC to repay your loan. It consists of two parts: the principal repayment and the interest payment. Our EMI Calculator Pro uses the standard banking formula to give you mathematically accurate results instantly.

The Mathematical EMI Formula

Whether you are taking a loan from SBI, HDFC, or ICICI, they all use this universal formula:

E = P × r × (1 + r)^n / ((1 + r)^n - 1)
  • E = EMI Amount
  • P = Principal Loan Amount
  • r = Monthly Interest Rate (Annual Rate / 12 / 100)
  • n = Loan Tenure in Months
